# Service Business Demo — Philip Builds Studio

A fictional one-page demo for a local service business, built by Philip Builds Studio.

## What this demo shows

- Sticky header with nav and CTA button
- Hero section with starting price and booking summary panel
- Three-tier pricing grid (Quick Visit $79 / Standard $149 / Full $249)
- Six trust-signal cards replacing made-up praise
- CSS-only before/after proof section (no stock images)
- Mobile-first quote form (demo only, no backend)
- Warm amber/cream design distinct from GlossLane (teal) and Crown & Fade (burgundy)

## Fictional brand

Example Service Co. is the fictional demo brand used on this page. No actual business content, customer data, or published outcomes are used.
