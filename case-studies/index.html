The template

Same structure every time, so nothing hides.

Every case study covers the same ground, in the same order:

  • Problem — what the project needed to prove or solve.
  • What I built — the shipped feature set, without inflation.
  • System design — the pipeline, with each stage labeled Live, Prototype, or Planned.
  • Security & claim-safety decisions — what the system refuses to do, and why.
  • QA evidence — the actual pre-release results, including what was caught and what was not covered.
  • Lessons — what carries into the next build.
  • Resume bullets & interview talking points — honest, specific, checkable against the live demo.
  • Next-version roadmap — labeled planned until it ships.
