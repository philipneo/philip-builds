Evaluation and security proof

The evidence is part of the product.

The public harness imports the same corpus and retrieval engine as the Secure RAG route. It checks both blocking and over-blocking, known-good rankings, honest failure modes, and structural safety contracts.

42 checks passing 9 refusal / 2 precision / 14 hit@k / 7 honesty / 10 contract
  • Private sources stay outside the corpus by allowlist construction.
  • External, private, and traversal-like result targets are rejected.
  • Corpus drift fails the build-time check before release.
  • Fallback, blocked, broad, and empty states remain explicit.

Problem

Decision
